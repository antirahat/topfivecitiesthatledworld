Rome: The Eternal City

Rome's legacy as the heart of the Roman Empire is evident in its enduring contributions to law, engineering, and architecture. The city's legal frameworks have influenced contemporary legal systems worldwide. Architectural marvels such as the Colosseum and aqueducts showcase advanced engineering techniques that have stood the test of time. Rome's rich history continues to captivate and inspire, earning it the moniker "The Eternal City."
